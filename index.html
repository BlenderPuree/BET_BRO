üìà Understanding Player Props

Player props are bets on individual player statistics like passing yards, rushing yards, receptions, or touchdowns. You bet whether a player will go OVER (more) or UNDER (less) a set line for tonight's only NFL matchup.

  • Line: The predicted stat for tonight's game
  • MORE: Player will exceed the line
  • LESS: Player will stay under the line
